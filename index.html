Features of TheImperialGod
We provide you the best features in TheImperialGod!
Moderation

TheImperialGod has powerful moderation features. These include kicking users, banning users, locking channels, unlocking channels and setting a custom amount of slowmode!

Moderation features are one of the best in all bots. Some commands help you even create roles by typing a command!

Economy

As everyone likes economy commands and fun, we have a good economy system for fun!

Given this it is your goal to make a ton of money in your account and keep trying to be the richest in your server!

Toggleable automoderation

Moderation features are good. If you have a server where swearing is banned and you don't have a ton of moderators online don't worry.

Because you have TheImperialGod with you! TheImperialGod, if automod is enabled will filter out all bad words and this only works in non NSFW channels.

TheImperialGod will also ban users who use racial slurs, but keep in mind moderators are immune to this!

Utilities

TheImperialGod has Utilities which are used by members who cannot use anything above! These are usually fun commands! But not as you would think of them!

Math Commands

C'mon sometimes everyone is a bit lazy to do maths and not everyone is gifted in mathematics.

Thus I have invented math commands for TheImperialGod to help you make sure your calculations are right!

Decimal calculations go up to 7 places in case you were wondering!

Fun

Fun commands, such as commands which automatically summon animals. They take a random animal from reddit and then show it to you on discord.

We have a number of animals and thus making your life easier.

Note: this is not limited to animals, we also have a meme command which shows you a random meme from reddit!
No more browing memes on reddit!

Giveaways

Yes, this bot is an all in one. Why have 10 bots in your server when one can do all?

We have an interactive gstart command which helps you plan a giveaway in your server!

We also have a reroll command to help reroll unfair giveaways!

Tickets

Most people are confused about what tickets are? But here is a simple explanation. Tickets is a way for users to contact the entire staff team!

Admins can setup a role of moderators who have the right to view any ticket.

Then users can create tickets with reasons and talk to staff!

Information

So, ever wanted to know something about a user or your server? The information category will keep you well informed about everything you'll want to know!

This can be used by both admins and moderators.
